1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Leap To Peak ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you play the game or interact with our services. By using Leap To Peak you accept the practices described here.

2. What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to run and improve the game:

  • Gameplay data: progress, scores, levels, in‑game events
  • Device data: device model, OS version, language, country
  • Technical identifiers: advertising IDs (IDFA/GAID) when ads are enabled
  • Analytics: session length, crash reports, performance metrics
We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health information, political opinions, or religious beliefs.

3. How we use data

We use collected data to:

  • Improve gameplay, fix bugs, and optimize performance
  • Understand player behavior to refine levels and balance
  • Deliver optional, relevant advertising if you opt in
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform policies

4. Third‑party services

We may use trusted third parties for analytics, advertising, and hosting. These providers process data under their own privacy terms. Examples include analytics platforms, ad networks, and cloud services.

5. Storage and security

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described. We apply industry standard safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing the personal data we hold about you
  • Requesting correction or deletion
  • Restricting or objecting to processing
  • Withdrawing consent for personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children

Leap To Peak is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
